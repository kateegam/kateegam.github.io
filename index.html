Kat Magee is a designer working around large cultural shifts/new tech and their influence on the human experience. She is currently working as a Graphic Designer (Brand–Creative) at Steelcase in Grand Rapids, Michigan. She is also exploring the capabilities of generative design and how it intersects with alternate experiences of the Internet. She has a professional background in visual design/direction + user-experience/sound design, with her most recent work focused in the furniture industry. Kat holds a BFA in Interactive Design (Digital Media) and Graphic Design from Kendall College of Art & Design.

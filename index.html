Kat Magee is a designer working around cultural shifts/new tech and their influence on the human experience. She is currently working as a Graphic Designer (Brand–Creative) at Steelcase in Grand Rapids, Michigan. She is also exploring the possibilities of generative design and its presumptive impact on alternate experiences of the internet. Kat has a professional background in UX, sound, + visual design — and holds a BFA in Interactive Design (Digital Media) + Graphic Design from Kendall College of Art & Design.

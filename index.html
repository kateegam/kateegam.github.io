Kat Magee is a designer working around tools in new tech and their influence on human culture. Her current focus is exploring the capabilities of generative design and how it intersects with alternate experiences of the Internet. She is currently working as a Graphic Designer (Brand–Creative) at Steelcase in Grand Rapids, Michigan. Kat holds a BFA in Interactive Design (Digital Media) and Graphic Design from Kendall College of Art & Design.
